About Us.

Founded in 2000, in the heart of the leafy suburbs of Loresho, Pizzeria is a pizza haven created to craft finger licking pizza with the taste of Italy. Our flavors are just like "a mother's taste."
With over 10 branches across the city of Nairobi, Our promise is to deliver yummy finger-licking pizza with locally sourced ingredients to satisfy your craving.

Our Values:

  • Finger-Licking Good.
  • Fast delivery.
  • Made to Perfection.
